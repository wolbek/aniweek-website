Contest Rules

How It Works

  1. Each week a random anime character is picked. Your job is to sketch that character by hand.
  2. Upload your sketch along with a short video (~10 seconds) of you drawing it. The video should show your hands and the sketch being drawn — it doesn't need to be a full timelapse, just enough to prove it's your work.
  3. The community votes on the submitted sketches. The top 3 sketches by votes win prizes!

Fair Play

  • No AI-generated art. Submissions found to be AI-generated or traced will be rejected.
  • No cheating. The drawing video is mandatory to verify authenticity. Submissions without a valid video will not be considered.
  • The contest is meant to be fair and fun. Admins reserve the right to disqualify any submission that violates the spirit of the contest.

Prizes

1st Place ₹500
2nd Place ₹200
3rd Place ₹100

How Winners Get Paid

Winners will receive an email congratulating them. To claim your prize, simply reply to that email with your GPay ID within 3 days. The payment will be sent to you directly.

Hall of Fame

The top 3 winning sketches from each contest are showcased in the Hall of Fame on our website and on our Instagram: @avanime_arts.
